    Jiwoo Moon (문지우) is a South Korean conceptual artist who also works in the medium of editorial / web / visual design, writings and photography.

    She relishes opportunies to explore these medium for the purpose of discovering ways to show proper context of the art she is working on.

    This website is a photographic project that she took on to document poetic journals of her time spent while on residencies in Seoul, Lyon, Berlin and also during her travels all over the world.
